About us

Test Development is recognized for innovative design, and passion for progress. As a development firm, Test has managed to stick to the original tenets upon which we were created. We are proud of the work we have done designing homes with families in mind, and commercial spaces for business owners. We take necessary risks to create extraordinary spaces that will enhance the buyers’ daily lives, and facilitate happiness.

Test is a leader of local urban development. As Chicago residents, we strive to energize and rejuvenate our communities. Test transforms housing, mixed-use developments, and commercial properties throughout Chicago to be modern and efficient.
